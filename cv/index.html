2020
Research Internship in Reinforcement Learning for Congestion Control
Nvidia AI Research

  • My internship was a joint project with Mellanox.
  • Mellanox provides network interface cards (NIC) and switches for datacenters and high performance computers.
  • One of the major obstacles in such systems is to prevent network congestion due to multiple servers sending at an overwhelming rate.
  • By tackling this problem with reinforcement learning, we learned an adaptive congestion control policy and improved performance across several metrics (such as fairness) when comparing to previous works.
